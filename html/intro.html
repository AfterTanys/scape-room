Nexum

It is the year 5000. Two millennia ago, an ancient deity known as the Visitor emerged from the wreckage of a crashed starship, bestowing upon humanity the gift of the Beacon, a force of pure light and creation. With this power, humans flourished, colonizing the entire solar system and basking in their golden age. However, this progress was halted abruptly by the emergence of a mysterious entity bearing an unstoppable curse, a power opposed to the Beacon: the Darkness. Calling himself the Hostage, he decimated human civilization, conquering planets and cities, bringing them to the brink of extinction. In a final act to preserve his chosen ones, the Visitor granted the power of the Beacon to a select group: the Beacon Bearers, who with their newfound powers managed to fend off the Hostage. But it was too late; humanity was in ruins, and the few who remained gathered in the last city of hope: Dawnstar. A thousand years after the fall, the threat of the Hostage looms anew, and a young woman holding the fate of humanity in her hands faces the chance to alter the course of history...
